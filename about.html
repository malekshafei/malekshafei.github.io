About Me

Background 1

The first football match I watched was Egypt losing to Algeria and failing to qualify for the 2010 World Cup. Despite the despair of the moment, the experience created a craving in my heart for Egyptian football. Although I have cloesley followed Premier League and MLS football over the years, I have always felt the strongest connection with following the careers of Egyptian footballers abroad. This motivated me to join the Egyptian sports website KingFut as a writer when I was 14 years old.
