About Me

Background

The first football match I watched was Egypt losing to Algeria and failing to qualify for the 2010 World Cup. Despite the despair of the moment, the experience created a craving in my heart for Egyptian football. Although I have cloesley followed Premier League and MLS football over the years, I have always felt the strongest connection with following the careers of Egyptian footballers abroad. This motivated me to join the Egyptian sports website KingFut as a writer when I was 14 years old. I began by covering the weekly performances of the Egyptians playing in Europe, from Mohamed Salah's historic first season at Liverpool to Ahmed Elmohamady's almost-constant cycle of promotions and relegations, I even got to interview Amro Tarek as he become the first Egyptian to sign for my local team - the New York Red Bulls.

I quickly grew a passion for broader issues relating to African football, especially the reasons why African football was so stunted in it's development compared to practically every other continent. This motivated me to create a long-form blog on African football called AfricanFtbl. I compiled a team of writers from almost every African country, and published over 100 features on a variety of topics ranging from the lasting impact of apartheid on South African football to ranking the top acaadmies on the continent. I highlighted many young players on the African continent, with the eye of shining a light on them ahead of potential moves to Europe.

Analytics

I developed a passion for football analytics in 2019. I experimented by highlighting the best statistical performers in the African Champions League, and gradually grew a keen interest in data recruitment. As was the case for many, the pandemic was a great opportunity to develop my skills and since then I have always been keeping a pulse on the developments in the industry; and at the same time, I am constantly thinking of ways to move the needle forward in advancing and spreading football analysis.

FC Midtjylland

I got my first opportounity to work in a football club in the summer of 2022, joining 3x Danish Champions FC Midtjylland as an intern in their scouting department. Within a few months, I was building the team's data scouting infrastrucutre from scratch. Since then, I have developed my presenting and analysis skills, and I am proud to say that I was the first to identify two signings that Midtjylland made in August 2023: 22-year-old right back Adam Gabriel from Czech outfit Hradec Kralove and 21-year-old centre back Han-beom Lee from Korean side Seoul FC.
